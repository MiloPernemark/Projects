Top 3 animes according to Milo Pernemark

My top 3 animes you will love

  1. A will eternal (一念永恒)

    A will eternal or (一念永恒) is a Chinese donghua which is their type of "anime". A will eternal is a evolving story around a optistic boy in the world of cultivation, magic and sects. You will excperience the main caracther Bai Xiaochun becoming stronger and stronger to lead his sect to sucess! It can be seen as a comedy but at dire moments you will most defenetly feel strong emotions of betrayl or compassion!

  2. Rurouni Kenshin (るろうに剣心)

    Kenshin's journey spans a decade of wandering across Japan as a rurouni, driven by a deep desire for redemption. He carries a sakabatō, a reverse-blade sword, with a solemn pledge to never take another life, a stark contrast to his past as an assassin for the Imperialist cause during the Bakumatsu era. In the year 1878, he finally arrives in Tokyo and finds a new home at the Kamiya Dōjō. Here, Kenshin faces countless challenges that put his vow to the test as he dedicates himself to preserving peace in the nation.

  3. Horimiya (ホリミヤ)

    At first glance, Kyouko Hori and Izumi Miyamura seem like an unlikely pair. Hori is the epitome of beauty and intelligence, while Miyamura comes across as quiet and distant. However, a chance encounter reveals their hidden sides. Hori, despite her popularity, is swamped with housework, leaving little time for friends. In contrast, Miyamura conceals his true self, marked by secret tattoos and piercings, giving off the appearance of a gentle delinquent, unbeknownst to his classmates.
